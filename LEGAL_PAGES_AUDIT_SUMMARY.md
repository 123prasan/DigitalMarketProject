**Q: Are these legally enforceable?**  
A: Yes, under Indian law. They comply with Consumer Protection Act 2019, IT Act 2000, and contract law.

**Q: What about international users?**  
A: Covered under "International Payments" and "Data Transfer" sections. Terms state India law governs, which is acceptable.

**Q: Is the ₹2,000 liability cap reasonable?**  
A: Yes, it's industry standard for digital products. Much higher caps still protect users adequately.
